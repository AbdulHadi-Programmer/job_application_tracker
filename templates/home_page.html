Frequently Asked Questions

What is Career Traces?

Career Traces helps you track your job applications, stay organized, and view analytics on your application progress.

How do I get started?

Click on the "Get Started" button and sign up to start tracking your job applications.

What features does Career Traces currently offer?

Manual tracking of job applications.

Analytics display through charts

Interview Scheduler based on user data

A simple, user-friendly interface with free access to all current features.

Is Career Traces free ?

Yes, Career Traces is currently free. We plan to introduce subscription-based advanced features in the future. .

Can I use Career Traces to find job opportunities?

No, Career Traces is not a job portal. It focuses on helping users organize and track job applications they apply for externally.

How do I reset my password if I forget it?

You can use the Forgot Password option on the login page, and a one-time password (OTP) will be sent to your registered email for verification.

Will my data remain private?

Yes, we value your privacy. All your data is securely stored and only accessible by you.

Can I export my job application data?

Export functionality is under consideration for future updates.

Is Career Traces mobile-friendly?

Yes, Career Traces is Responsive and work seamlessly on both desktop and mobile devices.
