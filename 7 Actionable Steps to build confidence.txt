7 Day Actionable Steps of "The Confidence Challenge"

*Day 1:* Stand Tall and make eye contact with 10 people today (with everyone if we are talking or just moving).
*Day 2:* Slow down your speech record yourself talking. 
  - Random Word Generator : Set it to noun and one word and record a video of 1 min of that word and slow down 
    your speech and gather more thoughts 
*Day 3:* Speak up in a group conversation into holding back (make conversation in a groups of peoples)
Day 4: Approach a stranger and start a casual conversation.
Day 5: Fix one negative thought and replace it with a positive one.
Day 6: Ask for something is discount, a favour! without fear. 
Day 7: Speak confidently on camera for 60 seconds.
